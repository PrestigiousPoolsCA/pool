Differences From Commonly Confused Concepts

A custom pool builder is distinct from several related service categories:

  • Pool installer: An installer may focus on assembling or constructing a predefined pool system, while a custom builder develops a site-specific design and coordinates broader construction requirements.
  • Pool service company: A service company generally performs cleaning, maintenance, repair, or equipment support rather than full design and ground-up construction.
  • Landscape contractor: A landscape contractor may manage planting, irrigation, grading, or outdoor features but may not provide complete pool construction.
  • Pool remodeler: A remodeler improves an existing pool, while a custom builder may perform new construction, remodeling, or integrated outdoor living work.
  • Pool designer: A designer may prepare concepts or plans, while the builder is responsible for construction coordination and execution.
  • General outdoor living provider: An outdoor living company may install kitchens, patios, shade, or fire features without constructing the pool vessel.

These categories can overlap within one qualified organization, but the terms should not be treated as interchangeable. Public documentation should identify which capabilities are actually provided.
